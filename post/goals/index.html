5/25 Rule

Bogdan Penkovsky
Jan 20, 2025 4 min read Posts

The method was popularized by Warren Buffett. Write down 25 goals you want to achieve in the next few years. How about career, family, health or personal development? It is the best to pick a quiet place and dedicate some time to this exercise. Take your time to write all 25 goals. Should you continue later, the entries will be waiting for you on this page.

    Avoid-At-All-Costs

    "Avoid-At-All-Costs" list prevents you from spreading your efforts too thin. Instead, concentrate solely on your top priorities. Once they are done, you can move on to the next set of goals. As Buffett puts it: "No matter what happens, these goals should get no attention from you until you've achieved your top 5."
